Refund Policy

Effective Date: 01-Jan-2025

Eligibility for Refund

  • 100% Refund is applicable only for first-time subscription of Premium or Ultimate plans.
  • Basic Subscription Plan is non-refundable.
  • 7-Day Refund Window: Full refund within 7 days from purchase or within 2 days from 3rd Live Class.
  • Subscription renewals are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, send an email to refunds@intliri.com with:

  • Full Name
  • Registered Email Address
  • Order ID or Transaction Number
  • Reason for the refund request (optional)

Processing & Refund Method

  • Refund requests are acknowledged within 3 business days.
  • Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.
  • Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method.

Trading Losses Refund Policy

  1. Trading Losses Refund policy is applicable only to students who have enrolled for Premium or Ultimate subscription plan of 10X Smart Trader Program.
  2. The terms and conditions, eligibility and applicability of Trading Losses Refund Policy is communicated to enrolled students during Program Induction.

Exclusions & Limitations

  • Refund eligibility may be voided if more than 50% of the content is accessed.
  • Violation of Terms or Community Guidelines will lead to refund rejection.

International Students

Refunds will be processed in the currency of the original transaction. Any transaction costs, bank charges, or forex markup charges will be borne by the student.

Updates to the Refund Policy

Intliri Academy reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. The latest version will be updated on this page.
